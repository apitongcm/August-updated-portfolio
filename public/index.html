Our Thesis in college

Wheelchair Auto-Navigational Drive with Electronic Response (project W.A.N.D.E.R)

The figure shows the concept of W.A.N.D.E.R a autonomous wheelchair. The device uses sensors for the safety feature of the patient. The control of the wheelchair will be manuever using the two switches and one push button. Switches and button are connected at the back upholstery of the wheelchair and will be responsible for every movements of the device. The relay module will isolates the external power supply for the motor that comes from a 24 volts with 2.8 ampere maintenance free lead acid battery.
